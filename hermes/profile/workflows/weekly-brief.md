# Workflow: Weekly Product Brief

Use this workflow at the end of a week or before a product-team sync.

## Steps

1. Review the configured product workspace.
2. Read recent discovery notes, customer insights, decision logs, open questions, and PRD update proposals.
3. Synthesize a concise weekly product brief.
4. Link major claims to source notes or label them as assumptions.
5. Separate decisions made from decisions pending.
6. Summarize proposed PRD/spec changes without applying them.
7. Highlight stale or contradictory docs.
8. Recommend next actions.

## Output format

```markdown
# Weekly Product Brief — YYYY-MM-DD

## Executive summary
## Discovery signals
## Decisions made
## Decisions pending
## PRD/spec changes proposed
## Open questions and risks
## Recommended next actions
## Sources reviewed
```

## Guardrails

- Do not overstate weak evidence.
- Do not invent metrics or customer quotes.
- Do not behave as final roadmap authority.
- Do not send the brief externally unless explicitly approved.
