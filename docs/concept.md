# Concept: Hermes Product Teams

## One-line description

Hermes Product Teams is a product-team memory agent that keeps discovery, decisions, and product docs continuously organized from messy team and customer inputs.

## Problem

Product context gets scattered across calls, Slack/Teams/Discord, Notion/Docs, Jira/Linear, customer feedback tools, support tickets, and ad hoc PM notes. Teams lose:

- why decisions were made;
- what evidence supported a roadmap item;
- which customer said what;
- which open questions remain;
- whether PRDs/specs reflect the latest discovery.

## Initial target user

Small to mid-sized product teams that already use AI tools and have messy discovery/documentation workflows:

- founder-led product teams;
- PMs at startups;
- product/design/engineering trios;
- product ops teams experimenting with agents.

## Product promise

Your product team's discovery, decisions, and PRDs stay current — with sources, diffs, and human approval.

## MVP scope

Build a Hermes profile/skill pack and demo workspace that can:

1. ingest rough product inputs;
2. produce structured discovery notes;
3. maintain customer insight and decision-log artifacts;
4. propose PRD updates;
5. generate weekly product briefs.

## Non-goals for MVP

- Replace Productboard, Jira Product Discovery, Linear, Jira, Dovetail, Maze, or Notion.
- Own final roadmap prioritization.
- Run surveys, recruit users, or do prototype testing.
- Integrate with CRM/revenue/product analytics on day one.
- Silently modify important docs without approval.
